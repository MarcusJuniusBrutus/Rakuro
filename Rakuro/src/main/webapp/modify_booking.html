Employee Booking Modifier

Here you can modify existing bookings. First, input the booking number of the booking you wish to modify in the left column and click on Submit. Then, the details of the booking will appear on the right column. Modify any parameters you wish and then click on Modify to finalize the changes. If you wish to not change anything, simply refresh the page. If you wish to delete the booking entirely, click on Delete. All changes/deletions are final.
